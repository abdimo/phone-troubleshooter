What is your problem?

. . .

  • Dead phone: phone does not switch on and has a blank screen
  • SIM card: sim card is not detected so cannot make calls
  • Hanging Problem: In this fault, the phone freezes frequently.
  • Network Problem: No signal and network or low network.
  • Microphone Problem: No out-going sound or sound is quiet.
  • Ringer Problem: No ringtone or music and loud speaker will not work.
  • Camera problem : back camera or front camera does not function
  • Vibrator Problem: No vibration.
  • Display Problem: No graphics on LCD or broken LCD, the display does not work.
  • Auto Switch Off: Phone switches off automatically even if not switched-off.
  • Restart Problem: Phone restarts automatically.
  • Call Cut Off: Call gets disconnected or cuts off.
  • Charging Problem: No charging or very slow charging on battery.
  • Keypad Problem: keypad doesn’t work or some keys do not function. Home button or volume buttons do not work.
  • Touch Problem: Touchscreen doesn’t work or slow touch on display.
  • Battery Discharge Problem: Battery gets drained very fast even when fully charged.
  • Bluetooth Problem: bluetooth does not work.
  • FM Radio: radio does not work or no tuning.
  • LED Problem: Not light on LCD.
  • Memory Card Problem: MMC does not get detected.
  • No Internet : internet does not work
  • WiFi Problem: WiFi does not function
